Nerding Out

This project got is inspiration from this Amazing Website Species in Pieces. Soon after its release I found an article by Smashing Magazine which provided a pretty detailed how-to guide

This inspired me to go back through my old adobe illustrator projects and see what I could make behave like this. Back in college, I took a Tai-Chi class and drew out the positions for a final project. A couple years later I found that drawing, and decided to built it out in Adobe Illustrator, I named it Warhol Tai Chi.

Now came the challenge of transforming this image as into vis-Ã -vis the style of Species in Pieces. 44 Triangles later I came up with this guy. With the basic structure conceived, I had to map out each triangle into its own div using ‑webkit‑clip‑path. 44 Divs later all the pieces are in play, now its time to animate it.

Animations are achieved through @‑webkit‑keyframes and essentially tweening the shape of each div to new coordinates and then back again. Most of the secondary animations were achieved in browser to see what felt right. Keeping track of all the coordinates easily starts to pile up and become a bit of a chore. If I ever re-visit this project I would make each coordinate a SASS variable ‑webkit‑clip‑path: polygon( VAR1 ,VAR2 ,VAR3 );

But alas hindsight is 20/20. All said and done it was a pretty excellent adventure in cutting edge browser technology
